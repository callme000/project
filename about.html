About TaskFlow

Empowering individuals to manage their time and tasks with simplicity.

Our Mission

TaskFlow was born out of a simple need: a task manager that is powerful enough for complex projects but simple enough for daily chores. We believe that productivity shouldn't be complicated. By focusing on a "Local-First" architecture, we ensure that your data is always accessible, fast, and private.

Whether you're a student managing assignments or a professional tracking project milestones, TaskFlow provides the visual clarity needed to stay focused and organized.

Privacy First

Your tasks are stored in your browser's LocalStorage. We don't track your data or store it on any external servers.

Simple Design

No bloated menus or unnecessary features. Just clean Kanban boards and a focused user interface.

Accessible

Built with Bootstrap 5 to ensure a fully responsive and accessible experience across all devices.
