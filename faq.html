How can we help?

Find answers to frequently asked questions and learn how to master your workflow.

TaskFlow uses Browser LocalStorage. This means your data is saved directly on your computer or device. No data is sent to our servers, ensuring total privacy and offline availability.

Currently, you can move tasks using the action buttons on each task card (e.g., "Start" or "Complete"). Drag-and-drop functionality is planned for a future update!

  • High: Critical tasks that need immediate attention.
  • Medium: Important tasks with standard deadlines.
  • Low: Minor tasks or ideas for the future.

Once a task is deleted, it is permanently removed from your LocalStorage to keep the application lightweight. Make sure you truly want to delete a task before confirming the prompt!
